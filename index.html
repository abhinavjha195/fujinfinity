About C2C Global Education

As a wholly-owned subsidiary of the prestigious C2C Global Education, Japan, we bring over 75 years of educational excellence to India. From our roots in Yamanashi Gakuin University, our mission has expanded across the globe, creating a network of top-tier educational institutions from preschools to universities, dedicated to fostering global citizens through a curriculum infused with our Japanese ethos of innovation and respect.

Our Blueprint For Global Education

Aligned with the United Nation's Sustainable Development Goals, we aim to become changemakers who actively engage with global issues, promoting sustainable development and world peace through education.

Our Vision For India

Launching with Fuji Infinity International Preschools, our entry into India marks the beginning of a significant expansion, blending the rich cultural values of respect, family, and wisdom that are deeply rooted in both Japan and India. We offer an educational experience that prepares children for a globalized future while respecting local tradition.

Our Journey

For more than seven decades, we've been at the forefront of educational innovation, consistently looking ahead.

1946

Established the Yamanashi Gakuin University in Kofu City, Japan.

1950

Yamanashi Gakuin Kindergarten was established.

1956

Yamanashi Gakuin Senior High School was established.

1962

Law/Department of Political Administration faculty was established.

1996

Yamanashi Gakuin Junior High School was established.

2004

Yamanashi Gakuin Elementary School was established.

2016

Sports Science faculty was established. Alumni from the Sports Science Programme won 2 Gold, 2 Silver, and 5 Bronze medals in 2020 Olympics.

2017

Fuji Infinity International Preschool was established in Vietnam

2019

Yamanashi Gakuin Kindergarten & Elementary School were certified as IB World Schools authorised to offer the Primary Years Programme (PYP).

2020

Fuji Infinity International Preschool was established in China.

2021

C2C Galactic Academy, which operates Milkyway Nursery Schools in Tokyo, Chiba, and Ibaraki, became a part of C2C Global Education.

2021

C2C Twinkle Academy, operating in Tokyo, Osaka, Wakayama, and Shiga become a part of C2C Global Education.

2023

The Montessori School of Tokyo became a part of C2C Global Education.
