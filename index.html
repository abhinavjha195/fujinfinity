About C2C Global Education India

As a wholly-owned subsidiary of the prestigious C2C Global Education, Japan, we bring over 75 years of educational excellence to India. From our roots in Yamanashi Gakuin University, our mission has expanded across the globe, creating a network of top-tier educational institutions from preschools to universities, dedicated to fostering global citizens through a curriculum infused with our Japanese ethos of innovation and respect.

Our Journey

An innovator in education for more than 75 years, always looking ahead.

In keeping with the educational founding spirit, the Furuya family has always pursued educational innovation to best meet the needs of students.

1946

Established the first Yamanashi Gakuin University in Kofu City.

1950

Yamanashi Gakuin Kindergarten was established.

1956

Yamanashi Gakuin Senior High School was established.

1962

Law/Department of Political Administration faculty was established.

1996

Yamanashi Gakuin Junior high School was established.

2004

Yamanashi Gakuin Elementary School was established.

2016

Sports Science faculty was established. Alumni from the Sports Science Programme won 2 gold, 2 Silver, and 5 Bronze medals in 2020 Olympics.

2017

Fuji Infinity International Preschool was established in Vietnam

2019

Yamanashi Gakuin Kindergarten & Elementary school were certified as IB World Schools authorised to offer the Primary Years Programme (PYP).

2020

Fuji Infinity International Preschool was established in China.

2021

C2C Galactic Academy was acquired by C2C Global Education and operates International Preschools across Tokyo, Chiba, and Ibaraki.

2021

C2C Twinkle Academy was acquired by C2C and extends its operations to Tokyo, Osaka, Wakayama, and Shiga, contributing to Fuji Infinity's expansion into childcare services.
