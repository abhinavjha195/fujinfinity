About Learning Paths SchoolA Few Words About Us

Xituxi International Education is a Chinese regional headquarters enterprise wholly owned by C2C Global Education Japan (referred to as C2C International Education Group).

It represents C2C International Education Group in promoting international student recruitment, international exchanges, Sino-Japanese cooperative education and other education projects in China, and also We will focus on promoting the concept of excellent preschool education and carrying out work around the operation of directly-affiliated daycare centers and kindergartens.

C2C International Education Group was founded in 1946 by Mr. and Mrs. Shinichi Furuya and Kiyoko, and is headquartered in Kofu City, the capital of Yamanashi, Japan. The group's subsidiaries include kindergartens, elementary schools, junior high schools, high schools, universities, junior colleges, and graduate schools. It is a well-known consistent private school education group in Japan. Its affiliated Yamanashi Gakuin University carries out exchanges and cooperation with 124 universities in 35 countries and regions around the world, bringing together more than 600 international students from more than 50 countries and regions, and the campus has a strong multicultural atmosphere.
