Founding Spirit

1946 - 1978
Showa 20 - 53

Our story begins with the founding of Yamanashi Gakuin University in 1946 by the Furuya Family, who believed that education was the catalyst for change to rebuild Japan postWorld War II. Located in Kofu City, in the shadow of Mt Fuji, the Furuyas recognised it as ‘the pinnacle of the school’ which would aspire students to achieve the highest level of education and character. The institution’s principles have ever since been tethered to Mount Fuji, and by extension, Japan and its culture.

  • 謙虚さ   Humility
  • 思いやりの心   Humanity
  • みんな一緒   Equality
  • 希望する力   Futuristic

Yamanashi Gakuin University, established in 1962

Building On The Legacy
In The Land Of The Rising Sun

1946 - 1978
Showa 20 - 53

From 1979, under the leadership of Tadahiko Furuya, YGU established a complete path to the finest education in Japan with six institutions from preschool through university.

  1. Yamanashi Gakuin University
  2. Yamanashi Gakuin College
  3. Yamanashi Gakuin Senior High School
  4. Yamanashi Gakuin Junior High School
  5. Yamanashi Gakuin Elementary School
  6. Yamanashi Gakuin Kindergarten

Yamanashi Gakuin Junior High School, established in 1996

Starting A Global Chapter With C2C

2018 - Present
Heisei 30 - Present

Three generations later, Mr Koji Furuya recognized the necessity for thinking and acting from insular to universal. Thus, C2C Global Education was formed to continue the legacy of educational excellence of the Yamanashi Gakuin institutions.

We have locations in Japan, China, Vietnam, and the USA. Our global footprint includes 120+ international partner schools, 4,000+ students, 50+ nationalities, and 70+ Olympians. Our international curriculum helps to develop individuals with multicultural perspectives

Our Philosophy

Driven by our Japanese ethos, we believe in “the right of children to be respected as human beings”.

We aim to provide children with a culturally respectful environment, an academically rich program, and the moral values, compassion, and love needed for the development of a healthy body, mind and spirit.

Our Vision (5Cs)

To build an outstanding early childhood learning community which will develop curiosity, creativity, confidence, compassion, collaboration, and will help children become life-long learners.

Our Mission (Every Child is Unique)

  • To provide learners with an enriching environment in which innovation, creativity and health are emphasized.
  • To help children reach their full potential, both academically and in preparation for life, through apt experiences.
  • To inspire children to achieve their best through collaborative learning in a safe and enjoyable learning environment.
  • To prepare confident and passionate learners who uphold the values of honesty, high moral standards, integrity and empathy.
