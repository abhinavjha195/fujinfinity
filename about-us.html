Founding Spirit

1946 - 1978

  • Yamanashi Gakuin University was founded by the Furuya Family in 1946.
  • The Furuyas believed education was vital for Japan's post-World War II reconstruction.
  • Located in Kofu City, near Mt. Fuji, the university aimed to inspire students towards academic and moral excellence.
  • YGU's principles are anchored in four core values
  • 謙虚さ   Humility
  • 思いやりの心   Humanity
  • みんな一緒   Equality
  • 希望する力   Futuristic

Yamanashi Gakuin University, established in 1962

Building On The Legacy

1979 - 2018

  • Under Tadahiko Furuya's leadership from 1979, YGU expanded into a comprehensive educational network.
  • The institution grew to encompass six entities, including preschools, schools, and colleges.
  • Yamanashi Gakuin University
  • Yamanashi Gakuin College
  • Yamanashi Gakuin Senior High School
  • Yamanashi Gakuin Junior High School
  • Yamanashi Gakuin Elementary School
  • Yamanashi Gakuin Kindergarten

Yamanashi Gakuin Junior High School, established in 1996

Starting A Global Chapter

2018 - Present

  • Mr. Koji Furuya, Chairman, initiated C2C Global Education in 2018, marking a shift towards a global presence.
  • C2C has its footprint in Japan, China, Vietnam, the USA and India.
  • The network comprises 120+ international partner schools.
  • It serves over 4,000 students from 50+ nationalities.
  • Alumni include over 70 Olympians.

Our Philosophy

Driven by our Japanese ethos, we believe in “the right of children to be respected as human beings”.

We aim to provide children with a culturally respectful environment, an academically rich program, and the moral values, compassion, and love needed for the development of a healthy body, mind and spirit.

Our Vision (5Cs)

To build an outstanding early childhood learning community which will develop curiosity, creativity, confidence, compassion, collaboration, and will help children become life-long learners.

Our Mission (Every Child is Unique)

  • To provide learners with an enriching environment in which innovation, creativity and health are emphasized.
  • To help children reach their full potential, both academically and in preparation for life, through apt experiences.
  • To inspire children to achieve their best through collaborative learning in a safe and enjoyable learning environment.
  • To prepare confident and passionate learners who uphold the values of honesty, high moral standards, integrity and empathy.

Chairman's Message

United by a common foundation rooted in Asian values, Japan and India share strong cultural, spiritual, and historical bonds. Fundamental principles such as respect, etiquette, and a deep appreciation for knowledge have intricately connected our societies.

Inspired by both Japan and Indian cultures, our educational endeavors will combine best of both cultures.

Mr Koji Furuya Chairman of C2C Global Education, Japan

Management Team

Mr Rana Dhiraj CEO and President, C2C Global Education India

Rana Dhiraj, President & CEO of C2C Global Education India, is a cultural bridge between Japan and India.

With a rich educational background and extensive experience in Japan spanning over 17 years, he brings home a wealth of knowledge and insight. His passion is evident in his commitment to introducing the finest Japanese educational and cultural practices to India, ensuring a harmonious integration and culturally enriched educational experience.

Mr Munehiro Nagao COO, C2C Global Education India

Having accumulated 28 years of experience, Mr Munehiro Nagao is a seasoned expert in sales and operations across international markets such as Japan, China, Southeast Asia, the US, and Europe.

With notable stints in London and Shanghai, he has honed his skills, particularly excelling in the Asian market. Currently, he is instrumental in advancing C2C's vision in India, contributing strategic acumen and operational proficiency to the expansion efforts.
